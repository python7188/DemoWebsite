Our Story

Born from a Passion for Excellence

TajHotel was founded with a singular vision: to redefine luxury dining in Delhi. We believe that exceptional food is not merely sustenance, but a profound experience — one that engages all the senses and leaves a lasting impression.

Every element of TajHotel has been thoughtfully curated — from the architecture that breathes warmth and elegance, to the menu that honours Indian culinary heritage while embracing global technique.

🏛️ Founded in Delhi 👨‍🍳 22 Years of Mastery ⭐ 5-Star Rated
The Artist

Chef Philosophy

Executive Chef

Chef Arjun Sharma

"Cooking is not just nourishment — it is storytelling. Every dish we plate at TajHotel carries the soul of its ingredients, the memory of its origin, and the aspiration of what Indian fine dining can become on the world stage."

With over 22 years of experience across Paris, Tokyo, Mumbai, and London, Chef Arjun brings global mastery and fierce Indian pride to every menu. His philosophy: honour the ingredient, respect the tradition, push the boundary.

Quality Sourcing

Farm to Table

Every ingredient that enters our kitchen is chosen with unwavering care. Our sourcing team visits partner farms quarterly — building real relationships with growers, auditing practices, and selecting only what meets our standards.
