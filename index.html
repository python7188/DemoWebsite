The Story Behind Our Signatures

Indian Classic

The Story Behind Butter Chicken

This legendary dish was born in the kitchens of Delhi in the 1950s, when chefs experimented with leftover tandoori chicken and a rich tomato butter gravy. Today at TajHotel, we honour that origin with slow-cooked chicken, house-churned cultured butter, and a velvety gravy simmered for six hours — making it one of the most celebrated dishes in Indian cuisine.

Signature Steak

The A5 Wagyu Story

Born on certified Japanese ranches at the foot of the Japanese Alps, our A5 Wagyu is flown in weekly. Its extraordinary marbling — a consequence of a meticulous 30-month raising process — delivers a butter-soft texture that dissolves on the palate. Seasoned only with truffle salt and served with aged red wine reduction, it is the pinnacle of our steak programme.

₹14,500 ★★★★★ 5.0
Royal Heritage

Hyderabadi Gold Biryani

A dish that carries the legacy of the Nizams of Hyderabad — the royal dum-pukht method seals fragrant saffron-laced basmati rice and tender meat together in a sealed vessel, allowing the steam and spices to meld into one extraordinary experience. At TajHotel, we finish each serving with a whisper of gold leaf — a tribute to its regal origin.

₹2,200 ★★★★★ 4.8
